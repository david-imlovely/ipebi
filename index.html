History

Ayanolu, known as Ayandugba founded ipebi community formally known as Ayandugba community in the 12th century. Ayanperuwa, his son Ayanolu, and his family migrated together with the sons of Oduduwa, Prince Oshifaderin and his brother Prince Oshile, members of their families and other princes and chiefs to found a kingdom.
